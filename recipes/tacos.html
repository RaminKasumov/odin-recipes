Tacos Recipe

Description

Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish consisting of small hand-sized corn or wheat tortillas topped with a filling. This recipe features a delicious combination of seasoned beef, fresh vegetables, and flavorful toppings, perfect for a quick and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 200ml tomato sauce
  • 8 small tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup salsa
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks.
  3. Stir in the taco seasoning and tomato sauce. Bring to a simmer and cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Warm the tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave.
  5. Assemble the tacos by placing a portion of the beef mixture onto each tortilla. Top with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, shredded cheddar cheese, sour cream, salsa, and fresh cilantro.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious tacos!

Enjoy!
