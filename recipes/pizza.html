Pizza Recipe

Description

Pizza is a classic Italian dish that is a family favorite. This recipe is a traditional pizza recipe that is made with a thin crust, rich tomato sauce, and gooey mozzarella cheese. It is a delicious and comforting dish that is perfect for a cozy night in.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup sliced onions
  • 1/4 cup sliced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup sliced basil leaves

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
  2. Roll out pizza dough on a floured surface to desired thickness.
  3. Place dough on a pizza pan or baking sheet.
  4. Spread pizza sauce over dough, leaving a 1-inch border.
  5. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over sauce.
  6. Top with Parmesan cheese, pepperoni, olives, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, and tomatoes.
  7. Bake in preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  8. Remove from oven and top with basil leaves.

Enjoy!
