Spaghetti Recipe

Description

Spaghetti is a classic Italian dish that is a family favorite. This recipe is a traditional spaghetti recipe that is made with a rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and gooey mozzarella cheese. It is a hearty and comforting dish that is perfect for a cozy night in.

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400g ground beef
  • 800g canned tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese, for serving
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the spaghetti and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks.
  4. Stir in the canned tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Bring to a simmer and cook for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce has thickened.
  5. Toss the cooked spaghetti with the sauce until well coated.
  6. Serve the spaghetti topped with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves.

Enjoy!
